Decentralized finance (DeFi) enables peer-to-peer lending, borrowing, and trading without intermediaries.
Smart contracts on Ethereum and other blockchains automate financial agreements.
Liquidity pools provide capital for decentralized exchanges and earn fees.
Governance tokens give holders voting rights in protocol decisions.
Stablecoins aim to maintain a peg to fiat currencies, providing stability.
Risk management strategies are essential due to volatility and smart-contract risk.
